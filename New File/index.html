Templates

Our website provides a comprehensive resource for creating effective presentation slides that engage and inform your audience. The Templates section features a range of professionally-designed PowerPoint templates, informed by research from cognitive science and design. These templates are optimized for the brain's natural processing and retention of information, incorporating features such as clear visual hierarchy, appropriate font usage, and use of contrasting colors. Whether you're a teacher looking to improve your classroom presentations or a business professional seeking to impress clients, our templates will help you to create visually appealing and effective presentations that engage and inform your audience.

About us

Our website is created by two students of Cognitive Science at Aarhus University, who are passionate about improving the learning experience for students. We recognize that lectures can be challenging to navigate, especially with the overwhelming amount of information presented in a short period. Therefore, we have developed this platform as a resource to help students enhance their learning experience by providing evidence-based principles from cognitive science and design. Our aim is to empower teachers and fellow students with the tools and knowledge needed to create engaging and effective presentations and study materials that maximize the takeaway from lectures. By sharing our expertise, we hope to create a community of like-minded individuals who are dedicated to improving the learning experience for themselves and others.
