Present take away

Break down the content of the lecture into salient points and provide the student with a take-away.
In the beginning of the lecture, the purpose of the lecture as well as the content’s link to former and further topics can be stated. To stimulate the student’s focused attention, it is helpful to specify what is expected to be learned from the lecture.
Furthermore, use midway summaries that are built on the main elements of the lecture, reviewing and repeating the information presented in a condensed manner.
Finally, utilizing the concept of summaries once again at the end of the lecture to capture the most salient points as a take-away.

How does this help my students?

1. Structure can focus the student's attention on salient points
2. Structure can guide note-taking
3. Midway summaries can provide a rest point to digest the information that have been delivered so far
4. Lecture slides and lecture videos can be used to study and prepare for exams
